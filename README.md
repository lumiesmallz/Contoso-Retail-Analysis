## Introduction

Contoso, a multinational corporation, leverages data analytics to enhance business performance across various sectors. Through detailed dashboards and reports, Contoso empowers its teams with actionable insights for strategic decision-making. During the analysis period, Akala Pelumi, a Data Analyst, contributed to developing comprehensive dashboards to monitor sales, orders, customer profiles, and geographic performance.

### Background

Based in multiple regions, Contoso operates a network of 307 physical stores and online channels, distributing products through various sales channels including stores, online platforms, resellers, and catalogs. The company collects data through standardized templates to analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) and collaborates with sales and marketing teams to ensure product availability and performance tracking.

#### Industry Type of Data

Retail / Sales Analytics

#### Stakeholders of the Analysis

1. Sales Representatives

2. Team Managers / Regional Sales Managers

3. Marketing Team

4. Sales Executives / Leadership Team

#### Value to Stakeholders

The dashboards provide real-time insights, enabling stakeholders to track performance metrics, identify trends, make informed decisions, and align operational strategies with business objectives.

#### Data Limitations or Biases

None
